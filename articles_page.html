How many points do I need to earn a Dining Cheque?

Date: July 17, 2013


You can redeem Dining Reward Points for OpenTable Dining Cheques which can be used at any OpenTable restaurant nationwide. You need a minimum of 2,000 points redeem a Dining Cheque. Dining Cheques are issued in three denominations, with the following points values.

OpenTable is the world's leading provider of online restaurant reservations, seating more than 12 million diners per month via online bookings across approximately 28,000 restaurants. The OpenTable network connects restaurants and diners, helping diners discover and book the perfect table and helping restaurants deliver personalized hospitality to keep guests coming back. The OpenTable service enables diners to see which restaurants have available tables, select a restaurant based on verified diner reviews, menus and other helpful information, and easily book a reservation.

How many points can I earn? - H2

Standard reservations earn 100 Dining Reward Points each. You can earn Bonus Points for reservations at select restaurants and times. Look for "1,000-point" reservations to accumulate dining rewards fast.

In addition to the company’s website and mobile apps, OpenTable powers online reservations for nearly 600 partners, including many of the Internet’s most popular global and local brands. For restaurants, the OpenTable hospitality solutions enable them to manage their reservation book, streamline their operations and enhance their service levels. Since its inception in 1998, OpenTable has seated more than 450 million diners around the world.

The Company is headquartered in San Francisco, California, and the OpenTable service is available throughout

  2. Canada
  3. Germany
  4. Japan
  5. Mexico
  6. UK

Where can I find my current points balance? - H3

Your Dining Points total is displayed on your OpenTable webpage and "My Profile" page when you log in. A message will alert you once you’ve accumulated enough points to redeem for a Dining Cheque.
